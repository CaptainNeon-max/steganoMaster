Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SteganoMaster

What is steganography?

Steganography is the practice of concealing information within other non-secret data or a physical object to avoid detection. Unlike encryption, which makes data unreadable but detectable, steganography hides the very existence of the secret information.

Is SteganoMaster free to use?

SteganoMaster offers a free version for personal use with all core functionalities. For advanced features and commercial usage, we offer premium licenses with additional capabilities and support options.

How secure is the data hiding process?

SteganoMaster uses advanced algorithms to embed data in ways that are extremely difficult to detect. When combined with our AES-256 or DES-256 encryption options, your hidden data is protected by multiple layers of security. However, remember that no security solution is 100% unbreakable.

Will hiding data affect the quality of my files?

SteganoMaster is designed to minimize any perceptible changes to the carrier files. For images and videos, the changes are typically invisible to the human eye. For audio files, the modifications are below the threshold of human hearing. However, very large hidden messages in small files might cause noticeable degradation.

How much data can I hide in a file?

The amount of data you can hide depends on the size and type of the carrier file. Generally, larger files can store more hidden data. SteganoMaster will automatically calculate and display the maximum capacity for each file you load.
